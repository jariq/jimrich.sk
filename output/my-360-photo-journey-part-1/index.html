I somehow came across a YouTube videos showcasing a Xiaomi Mi Sphere camera (no longer available) and I immediately knew it was the thing I was looking for. I bought it and it quickly became one of my favorite photography related tools ever.

I don’t bother with adjusting the camera settings, I just push the button on a selfie stick, camera in AUTO mode beeps and that’s it - my memory of the location is captured and recorded on the memory card. When I come back home I copy all the photos to my computer, choose the best ones and publish them.
